How to Play

Objective

Collect all 10 Dragonstalker tier set pieces to win! Missing any piece means game over.

Controls

Desktop: Mouse to move your character

Mobile: Touch screen to move your character

ESC: Pause game

S: Open/close settings

Beneficial Spells (Collect These!)

Power Word Shield - Grants temporary shield protection
Frost Nova - Freezes all projectiles (2 HP damage)
Speed Boost - Increases game speed for more points

Player Spells

Desktop: Press Q/W/E keys | Mobile: Touch the spell icons!

Q - Dragon Cry: Move anywhere on screen + 5% crit chance (1 min cooldown)
W - Zandalari: +20% points, slow projectiles and items (45s cooldown)
E - Flask of Titans: Remove one missed Dragonstalker (2 min cooldown)

Dangerous Projectiles (Avoid These!)

Fireball - 8 HP damage
Frostbolt - 15 HP damage
Shadowbolt - 10 HP damage over 5 seconds

Item Types & Points

Zee Zgnan: 15 points (Ultra Rare)
Legendary: Thunder Fury - 5 points + Auto-collect all items
Tier Set: Dragonstalker pieces - 6 points each (Must collect all 10!)
Special: 4-7 points (Rare items)
Epic: 5-10 points (Uncommon items)
Regular: 1-4 points (Common items)

Critical Hit System

10% base chance to double points from any item

Complete Dragonstalker sets: +5% crit chance each

Collect Thunder Fury: +1% crit chance each

Maximum crit chance: 25%

Dodge Rating System

10% base chance to avoid damage from projectiles and item misses

Complete Dragonstalker sets: +1% dodge chance each

Zandalari spell: +5% dodge chance for 15s after spell expires

Aspect of the Monkey power-up: +3% dodge chance for 15 seconds

Evasion power-up: +1% permanent dodge chance

Area Expansion: Each HP saved from dodges expands your movable area by 1 pixel

Dodge is checked BEFORE Power Word Shield

Dodge does NOT prevent DOT damage ticks

Maximum dodge chance: 25%

Arrow Combat System

Collect "Thorium Headed Arrows" power-ups to gain 1000 arrows

Autoshot (A): Fires 1 arrow straight up (no cooldown)

Multishot (R): Fires 3 arrows in spread pattern with +5% crit (10s cooldown)

Arrows collect items on contact (same as player collision)

Arrows destroy harmful projectiles and gain beneficial effects

Multishot arrows have increased crit chance for collected items

Scoring Tips

Use Zandalari spell for +20% points and to slow down all falling objects

Collect Speed Boost projectiles to increase game speed and scoring

Thunder Fury automatically collects all items on screen AND increases crit chance

Complete multiple Dragonstalker sets to maximize crit chance

Higher game speeds = more items = higher scores
